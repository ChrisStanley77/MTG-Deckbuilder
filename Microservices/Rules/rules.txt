Commander Rules
---------------
1. Deck Size = 100
2. Must have min of 1 max of 2 Commander(s)
3. All cards must share colors with the Commander(s)
4. Only 1 copy of each card is allowed, except for basic lands



Modern Rules
------------
1. Deck Size = 60
2. No more than 4 copies of each card are allowed, except for basic lands
3. Sideboard = 15 max
